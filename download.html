Installation Guide

New to custom ROMs? Follow our step-by-step guide to install havocOOS on your device.

1

Backup Your Data

Before flashing, make sure to backup all your important data using TWRP or your device's built-in backup solution.

2

Unlock Bootloader

Enable OEM unlocking and Developer Options, then unlock your bootloader using fastboot or your manufacturer's tool.

3

Flash Recovery

Download and flash a custom recovery (TWRP or OrangeFox) compatible with your device for easy installation.

4

Wipe & Flash

Perform a factory reset, then flash the havocOOS ROM zip file along with GApps if desired.

5

Reboot & Enjoy

Reboot your device and wait for the first boot. Setup your device and enjoy havocOOS!
