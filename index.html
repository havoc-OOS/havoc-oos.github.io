About havocOOS

havocOOS is a custom Android ROM based on the latest Android source code, designed to provide a clean, fast, and feature-rich experience. Our team of dedicated developers works tirelessly to bring you the best possible Android experience.

We believe in open-source software and community-driven development. Our ROM is built with passion and attention to detail, ensuring that every user gets a premium experience without any bloatware or unnecessary modifications.
